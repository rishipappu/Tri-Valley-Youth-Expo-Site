What is the Tri-Valley Youth Expo?
The Tri-Valley Youth Expo is an exposition of the arts and culture of the youth in the Tri-Valley. It will be a showcase of the best and the greatest of what youth in the Tri-Valley are able to offer. The Expo will be a multidimensional event, combining together culture, the youth, the arts, and coding into one event.

Dublin High School, Dublin, CA
